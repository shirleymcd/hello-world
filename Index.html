Create a Digital Signature for Use with Forms

​​The following procedure describes the steps to create a digital signature for use with forms.

  1. Use a black or blue ballpoint pen to sign your name on a white sheet of paper.
  2. Use your phone camera to take a photo of your signature.
  3. Send the image file to your email address.
  4. Save the image file on your computer.
  5. Use Word to add the signature to a form, Insert > Pictures, and navigate to your saved file.
  6. Resize the image, if necessary.
  7. Use the Crop option in Word after you insert the image, if required.
